Dhan-Lakshmi: AI-Powered Financial Chatbot

Dhan-Lakshmi is a cutting-edge ChatGPT-powered Financial AI Chatbot designed to revolutionize bank statement analysis. This innovative tool combines the power of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and artificial intelligence to provide users with intuitive, conversational interactions with their financial data.

Key features of Dhan-Lakshmi include:

  • Intelligent parsing and analysis of bank statements
  • Detection of fraudulent activities and unusual spending patterns
  • Categorization of expenses for better financial management
  • Personalized financial insights and recommendations
  • Natural language queries for easy information retrieval

The project leverages advanced NLP techniques and machine learning algorithms to understand and respond to user queries about their financial data. By making financial information more accessible and understandable, Dhan-Lakshmi aims to empower users to make informed financial decisions and improve their overall financial health.
