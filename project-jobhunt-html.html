JobHunt360: Advanced Job Market Analytics Dashboard

JobHunt360 is a comprehensive job market intelligence platform designed to revolutionize the way job seekers and market researchers interact with employment data.

Key features include:

  • Precision Job Matching: Utilizes advanced filtering algorithms to connect candidates with highly relevant job opportunities, including niche positions.
  • Market Insights Generator: Provides researchers with real-time job market analytics, enabling trend identification and demand forecasting.
  • Interactive Data Visualization: Presents complex job market data through intuitive, interactive charts and graphs for easy interpretation.
  • Customizable Alerts: Allows users to set up personalized notifications for new job postings or market shifts in their areas of interest.
  • Industry Comparison Tools: Enables side-by-side analysis of job markets across different industries and regions.

This project demonstrates proficiency in data analytics, user experience design, and building scalable web applications for real-world problem-solving.
