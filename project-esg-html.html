ESG Scoring Framework: Comprehensive Corporate Sustainability Assessment Tool

The ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) Scoring Framework is a sophisticated analytical tool designed to evaluate and quantify a company's performance in key sustainability and ethical practices. This framework serves as a critical component in modern investment strategies and corporate risk assessment.

Key features include:

  • Multi-dimensional Analysis: Assesses companies across Environmental, Social, and Governance factors, providing a holistic view of corporate sustainability.
  • Risk Management Metrics: Generates scores that serve as indicators of a company's long-term viability and risk exposure.
  • Investor-focused Insights: Tailored for B2B investors, offering valuable data for informed decision-making in investment portfolios.
  • Credit Risk Integration: Incorporates ESG factors into traditional credit risk models, enhancing the accuracy of financial risk assessments.
  • Customizable Weighting: Allows for adjustment of factor importance based on industry-specific concerns or investor priorities.
  • Trend Analysis: Tracks ESG performance over time, enabling the identification of improvement or deterioration in corporate practices.

This project showcases expertise in financial modeling, data analytics, and the development of decision-support tools for the investment sector. It demonstrates a deep understanding of sustainable business practices and their impact on corporate performance.
